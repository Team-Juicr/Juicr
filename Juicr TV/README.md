# Juicr TV

Juicr TV is the remote-first Android TV experience for Juicr. It brings Juicr's browsing, library, and playback surfaces to large screens with layouts shaped for directional controls.

## Highlights

- Home, Discovery, Library, and Settings designed for TV navigation.
- Large focus targets and readable spacing for couch-distance use.
- Browse available movies, series, animation, and Live TV when enabled options support them.
- Keep setup user-controlled, so fresh installs stay empty until users choose what to enable.
- Share support evidence through redacted diagnostics instead of private account or playback details.

## Development

Use the focused Juicr TV checks documented in the workspace scripts before opening or merging TV changes. Keep TV updates remote-friendly, readable, and aligned with the shared Juicr privacy posture.
